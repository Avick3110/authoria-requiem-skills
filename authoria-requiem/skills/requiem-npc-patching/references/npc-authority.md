# NPC Authority — every Requiem-lane plugin that touches NPC_ records

The authority for NPC balance is **the live conflict winner across the whole Requiem lane**, not
`Requiem.esp` alone. Enumerated live (2026-07-17, ~18.5k NPC_ records in the order): seven
hand-authored Requiem-lane plugins touch actors, and the right **analogue source depends on what
kind of actor you're patching**. On a live consumer profile, `Requiem for the Indifferent.esp`
(the Reqtificator's output) folds the build pass over all of them and is the winner to derive
from; the table below is the hand-authored layer beneath it.

| Plugin | NPCs touched | Defines new | What it owns (→ when it's your analogue source) |
|---|---|---|---|
| `Requiem.esp` | 2702 | 339 | the base rebalance — nearly every vanilla combat actor, the `REQ_*_Template_*` bases, named civilians' stat passes. Default analogue source. |
| `Requiem_VampireCollection.esp` | 371 | 231 | the vampire overhaul — vampire NPCs, tiers, traits. **Any vampire actor derives from here.** |
| `Requiem - Minor Arcana - Forsworn.esp` | 225 | 183 | the forsworn ladder. **Forsworn-archetype enemies derive from here.** |
| `USMP - Requiem.esp` | 128 | 0 | pure forward of Requiem's values over USMP's bugfix layer — the live winner for many named actors; reading it *is* reading Requiem. |
| `Requiem - Magic Redone.esp` | 66 | 41 | caster **summons** (atronachs, thralls, conjured actors). **A summoned actor's analogue lives here.** |
| `Requiem - Weapons and Armor Redone.esp` | 59 | 15 | re-tempers actors' gear traits (tempering `ActorEffect` swaps). |
| `Requiem - Resist and Regen Tweak.esp` | 4 | 0 | single-perk physique injections (`000805` fur etc. — the trait-bridge taxonomy). |

Reading the table when picking an analogue:

- **Pick the lane before the actor.** A modded vampire boss compared against a `Requiem.esp`
  bandit gets the wrong everything — its peers live in `Requiem_VampireCollection.esp`. Likewise
  forsworn → Minor Arcana; a conjured/summoned actor → Magic Redone's summon set.
- **A "touch" is not always balance.** WAR's 59 touches are mostly tempering-trait swaps; RRT's 4
  are single perks. Read the conflict tree's field diff to see *which* plugin owns the balance
  fields on your comparable, and derive from that winner.
- **The big post-Requiem NPC winners in a curated order are tool outputs** (`Authoria -
  Reqtificated - *`, NPC merge plugins, `Requiem for the Indifferent.esp`) — regenerated by their
  tools. Deriving *values* from a live winner is correct (the merge folds Requiem's balance in),
  but never treat a tool-output plugin as a hand-authored addon lane or copy its records
  wholesale into a patch (see the `tool-output-awareness` doctrine).
- New Requiem addons appear; the enumeration recipe to refresh this table is one call:
  `housecarl_cross_plugin_query type="NPC_" group_by="winner"` (then per-plugin
  `plugins=["<addon>"] type="NPC_"` for touch counts).
